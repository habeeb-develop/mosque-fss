About FSSSMC Mosque

FSSSMC Mosque is a center for worship, learning, and community development. We are committed to serving Allah and supporting Muslims through spiritual growth, education, and unity.

Our Mosque

Our mosque provides a peaceful environment for daily prayers, Jummah services, Islamic programs, and community gatherings.
